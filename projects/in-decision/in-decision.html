InDecision

Big decisions rarely come with clear answers. That’s why InDecision helps you find clarity by turning gut feelings into something visible, audible—even recordable. It presents you with options to react to, providing a space to discover what matters in a process that builds meaning over time.

Options & Criteria

Think aloud.

You already know what matters. You just haven’t heard yourself say it yet. InDecision helps you get there, and discover more along the way, by presenting contrasting options designed to surface your intuition, your values, and the tradeoffs you didn’t know you were making.

We don’t always know what we’re optimizing for when we make a decision. InDecision helps surface that—by listening to how you respond to options and turning those reactions into structured, editable criteria.

Just tap to edit or weigh criteria.

Why does this matter to you? InDecision prompts reflection on your top priorities by asking questions. You might realize two Must-Haves are pulling in different directions. Or that what you thought was about money… is really about peace of mind.

Decision ID

Save progress.

Every decision in InDecision ends with a Decision ID. It's a snapshot of what matters to you in a particular decision and why. Use them to help jumpstart a new one with built-in context. Or use a friend’s to co-navigate something big, like moving in together or planning for retirement.

Go back to the drawing board. You can always return to earlier parts of your decision: revisit the options, reshape your criteria, or re-sort what matters most. Every pass brings you closer to what feels right.

What else could you be deciding? InDecision can suggest related choices by remixing pieces of your own thinking across decisions.

Some decisions aren’t yours alone to make. You can upload someone else’s DecisionID to understand where they’re coming from—or invite them to contribute directly to yours.
